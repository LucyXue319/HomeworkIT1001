NUS NextBus

App for NUS Students

About

The app I will be reviewing today is most likely one most of you are familiar with - NUS NextBus. The link for this app can be found here. This app provides up-to-the-minute bus schedules and tells you ahead of time the arrival time of buses. Detailed maps of bus stop locations and user's current location within NUS is also provided.

I personally use this app daily whenever I am travelling to class or the MRT station. I find that the app does do the job it is advertised to do and it is I like how it is usually pretty accurate with the estimations. In general, I would say that I like the app, however some improvements I would suggest would be to improve the speed of the location service. Sometimes it takes up to one minute to load so it could be made more efficient using a manual search option.
