About

Hi everyone! I come from the wonderfully rainy but picturisque Vancouver in British Columbia, Canada. I chose Singapore - the Lion City as the destination for my exchange because I wanted to escape from the cold, harsh Canadian winter and go somewhere super sunny. Singapore has definitely exceeded my expectations in that respect and also has introduced me to some amazing new food that I can now safely call my favourites!

I used to be an accounting major, but after completing an internship doing audit work I have now switched over to a marketing and management information systems double concentration with a certificate in business analytics and decision making.In my spare time, I like to go to the gym, watch Netflix, and hang out with my friends.
